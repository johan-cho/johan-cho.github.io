mbta mapper

realtime mbta tracking

 

I used to work for Keolis; our team developed the backend of a web app that mapped out the MBTA's Commuter Rail service that the company runs in real-time. It was an internal app that I became reliant on to commute into Boston, and I knew that I'd lose access to it when I left the company, so I made my own public-facing version that I use every day.

I use sqlalchemy in the backend and leaflet.js to render the map. I deployed it to digitalocean using docker.
